Purpose

Investors are often left holding the bag when DeFi projects do not go as planned. Of course, we should always do our own research but there is still a chance things will not work out as we hope.
Even projects that seem stable can sometimes come crashing down (see Luna)
The Dead Token Exchange allows holders of low value, unsellable or unwanted tokens to exchange them for DTE tokens. DTE tokens can then be held for long term value, staked to earn interest or swapped for BNB (24 hour cool down for BNB swap).

The Dead Token Swap

The Dead Token Swap allows holders of Dead Token Exchange tokens to swap their useless tokens (abandoned projects, rugged projects or unwanted tokens) for DTE tokens. The DTE tokens have actual value and can then be held as their value increases, swapped for BNB or exchanged for some of our vetted partner projects.

DTE VAULT

The DTE Vault is an integral part of the Dead Token Exchange ecosystem. It provides additional funding to support the exchange of dead or unwanted tokens for DTE tokens. A portion of the Vault fee will also be used for investments in stable coins and thoroughly vetted partner projects. As these projects grow so will the value and financial security for our holders. The profits from these investments will be used for strategic buy backs of DTE as well as direct airdrops to our holders.

FAQ's

Is this project is safe?

The purpose of the DTE is to help those that have been affected by other abandoned or scam projects. We have taken steps to ensure the project is safe such as locking liquidity and several contract audits.

How much DTE must be held in order to swap dead tokens?

Holders must have a minimum of 200,000 DTE tokens in their wallet in order to utilize the DTE swap.

Why is there a 24 hr waiting period to sell swapped DTE?

The 24 hr waiting period only applies to DTE that is acquired from swapping Dead tokens. This is a safety measure to protect the price stability of the exchange from dumps

What are vetted projects?

Our partner projects are vetted recommendations for our community members. These projects are thoroughly screened and researched prior to listing on our site.
